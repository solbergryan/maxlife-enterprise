# Image Attributions

This document lists the sources and licenses for all images used on MaxLife Realty's website.

## Pixabay Images

All images in the `commercial-stock/` directory are sourced from Pixabay (https://pixabay.com). Pixabay images are released under the Pixabay License, which allows free use for commercial and non-commercial purposes without requiring attribution, though attribution is appreciated.

### License Summary

- **License**: Pixabay License (Free use)
- **Attribution**: Not required, but appreciated
- **Restrictions**: Cannot be used to compete with Pixabay or used to create a similar service
- **Original Source**: https://pixabay.com

### Commercial Stock Subdirectories

Images are organized by category in subdirectories:

- `about-broker/` — Broker/team imagery
- `careers/` — Career/team collaboration imagery
- `investors/` — Investor and business imagery
- `lease-types/` — Contract and lease-related imagery
- `marketplace/` — Listing and marketplace imagery
- `mixed-commercial/` — Mixed-use and multi-tenant commercial properties
- `office-buildings/` — Office and office-park imagery
- `professionals/` — Professional services and business imagery
- `retail-storefronts/` — Retail and storefront imagery
- `special-purpose/` — Special-use property imagery
- `glossary/` — Educational and glossary-related imagery

### Downloaded via Scripts

Images in these directories were downloaded using the `scripts/download_pixabay_landing_pages.py` and `scripts/download_pixabay_missing_categories.py` scripts, which pull from Pixabay's API. Each image retains its original Pixabay ID embedded in the filename for reference.
