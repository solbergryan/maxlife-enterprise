**The Property:**
Multi-tenant retail strip center, East Orlando (near UCF corridor). Built 2008, 12,400 SF, 4 units, fully occupied.

**The Numbers:**

| Annual Debt Service | $89,400 |

| DSCR | 1.53x |

**Why this deal worked:**

1. **Below replacement cost.** At $133/SF, you couldn't build this today for under $200/SF. That's a built-in margin of safety.

2. **Tenant mix.** Two national tenants (quick-service restaurant + medical clinic) anchoring 60% of the income, plus two local service tenants with 3-year leases. Blended credit quality is strong.

3. **Location.** UCF corridor has 72,000+ students plus the Research Park employment center. Vacancy in this submarket runs 3–5%.

4. **Value-add upside.** One unit was 15% below market rent on a lease expiring in 8 months. Renewal at market rate pushes NOI to $145,000+ and the effective cap to 8.8%.

**The takeaway:**
Not every deal is a 5-cap NNN with Walgreens. Sometimes the best risk-adjusted returns come from well-located multi-tenant retail where you're buying below replacement cost with room to push rents.
