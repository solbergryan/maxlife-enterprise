## Posting Schedule Recommendation

| Day | Template | Topic Focus |
|-----|----------|-------------|
| Monday | Market Stat Drop | Lead with data from the prior week |
| Wednesday | Deal Breakdown OR Myth Buster | Alternate weekly |
| Friday | Investor Question Answered | Source from DMs, emails, or common client questions |

**Best practices:**
- Post between 7–9 AM EST (peak LinkedIn engagement for B2B/finance)
- Always include 3–5 relevant hashtags at the end
- Put links in the FIRST COMMENT, not the post body (LinkedIn suppresses posts with outbound links)
- Tag relevant locations (@Orlando, @Central Florida) when applicable
- Engage with every comment within the first hour
