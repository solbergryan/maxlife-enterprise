## Carousel Template 1: "By The Numbers" (5–7 slides)

**Slide 1 — Hook**
- Background: Dark (#0a0a0a) with gold (#EFBF04) accent line
- Large bold text: "[Surprising stat or question]"
- Examples:
  - "Orlando added 80,000+ new residents last year."
  - "What cap rate should you expect in Central Florida?"
  - "The #1 reason out-of-state investors choose Florida"
- Small text at bottom: "@maxlifedevelopment" + "Swipe →"

**Slide 2–5 — Data Points (one per slide)**
- Background: Dark with subtle gradient
- Large number or stat in gold
- 2–3 lines of context in white
- Example:
  - Gold: "5.5% – 6.5%"
  - White: "Average cap rate for NNN retail in Orlando with national credit tenants"
  - Gray subtext: "Source: MaxLife market analysis, Q1 2026"

**Slide 6 — Summary Takeaway**
- Background: Dark
- White text: "[Key insight or actionable takeaway]"
- Example: "Central Florida's fundamentals are as strong as they've been in a decade. The question isn't whether to invest — it's where."

**Slide 7 — CTA**
- Background: Dark with gold border
- Text: "Follow @maxlifedevelopment for weekly Central Florida market intel"
- Subtext: "Link in bio for off-market deal access"
- MaxLife logo

## Carousel Template 2: Property Showcase (5 slides)

**Slide 1 — Property Hero**
- Property photo (or clean data visualization) with dark overlay
- Overlay text: "[Property Type] — [Location]"
- Gold accent bar at top
- Badge: "[Cap Rate]% Cap" or "[Status — e.g., Just Listed, Under Contract]"

**Slide 2 — Key Financials**
- Dark background, clean layout
- Two columns:
  - Left: Purchase Price, NOI, Cap Rate
  - Right: Cash Flow, Cash-on-Cash, DSCR
- All numbers in white, labels in gray
- Gold divider line between columns

**Slide 3 — Location Context**
- Map graphic or submarket description
- Key location highlights:
  - "[X] miles from [major anchor/employer]"
  - "[X] daily traffic count"
  - "Population within 3-mile radius: [X]"
  - "Median HH income: $[X]"

**Slide 4 — Why This Deal Works**
- 3–4 bullet points in white on dark background
- Gold checkmark icons
- Examples:
  - "Below replacement cost at $X/SF"
  - "National credit tenant, 12 years remaining"
  - "2.5% annual rent increases"
  - "Located in top-3 growth corridor"

**Slide 5 — CTA**
- "DM 'DEALS' for access to our off-market inventory"
- "@maxlifedevelopment"
- "Link in bio"

## Reel Script Template: "60-Second Market Update"

**[0:00–0:03] HOOK**
- On camera or text overlay on B-roll
- "Here's what's happening in Central Florida CRE this week."
- Alt hooks:
  - "Three things CRE investors need to know this week."
  - "Orlando's commercial market just did something interesting."
  - "If you're investing in Florida real estate, watch this."

**[0:03–0:48] BODY — 3 Data Points**

Data Point 1 (15 sec):
- "[Topic] — [Stat or news item]"
- "[Brief commentary on what it means for investors]"
- On-screen text overlay reinforcing the key number

Data Point 2 (15 sec):
- "[Topic] — [Stat or news item]"
- "[Brief commentary]"
- On-screen text overlay

Data Point 3 (15 sec):
- "[Topic] — [Stat or news item]"
- "[Brief commentary]"
- On-screen text overlay

**[0:48–1:00] CLOSE**
- "Follow for weekly Central Florida CRE updates."
- "Link in bio for off-market deal access."
- Point to "Follow" button or gesture

**Caption template:**
```
Central Florida CRE update — [date or "this week"]:

1️⃣ [Data point 1 — one line]
2️⃣ [Data point 2 — one line]
3️⃣ [Data point 3 — one line]

[One sentence takeaway]

Follow @maxlifedevelopment for weekly updates.
Link in bio for off-market deal access.

#CREUpdate #OrlandoRealEstate #CommercialRealEstate #FloridaInvestor #MarketUpdate #RealEstateInvesting #CentralFlorida
```

**Production notes:**
- Film in office, at property, or use B-roll of Central Florida commercial properties
- Use captions/text overlays — 85% of Instagram video is watched without sound
- Keep energy conversational and confident, not scripted/corporate
- Post Reels Tuesday–Thursday between 11 AM–1 PM EST for best reach
